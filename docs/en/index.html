Institute for Unified Higher‑Dimensional Information Physics (IU‑HIP)

Toward a Unified Theory of Higher‑Dimensional Information Physics

IU‑HIP is an independent research institute dedicated to understanding information, geometry, and physical laws within higher‑dimensional hierarchical structures.

Our primary mission is to develop and systematize the R‑layer Mode Theory (RLMT), aiming to reveal the deep informational structures underlying the universe.
