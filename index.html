About (English Preview)

R-layer Mode Theory (RLMT) is a new theoretical framework that aims to provide a unified explanation for some of the most fundamental phenomena in the universe, including black holes, dark matter, dark energy, gravity, the fundamental forces, quantum–cosmic connections, quark patterns, and early-universe dynamics.
